Tell us if you’re staying at home because of coronavirus

This is a research tool to help us record how many people are staying at home due to coronavirus (COVID-19).

By answering some quick questions you can help the NHS to understand:

  • how people are being affected by coronavirus
  • the number of people recovering from coronavirus
  • which areas of the country need resources most
Information:

We will not share the answers you give us beyond the Department of Health and Social Care.

If you tell us your mobile number we can text you daily advice and updates.

If you complete this survey for someone else, you’ll need to tell us their details.
