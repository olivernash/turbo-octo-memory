Help slow the spread of coronavirus in your community

This app can help protect you and the people around you from coronavirus (COVID-19).

It does this by alerting you if you’ve spent time near someone who is later diagnosed with the virus, if they are also using this app.

If enough people in your community use this app and follow the advice, the spread of coronavirus can be slowed.
