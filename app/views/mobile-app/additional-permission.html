How we’ll use data you share with us

This app records your device’s location and whether there are other nearby devices using the same app.

So we can trace people who may have been exposed to coronavirus and slow the spread of it, we need to store this data in one place.

Your data will not be shared beyond the NHS, and will not be used for any purposes beyond studying how coronavirus is spreading.
