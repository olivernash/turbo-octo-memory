Check your real-time coronavirus risk

This app can help you to protect yourself and the people around you from coronavirus (COVID-19).
It could also help stop the spread of coronavirus in your community.

It can tell you:

  • Your personal risk of catching coronavirus, based on where you’ve been and who you’ve been in contact with
  • Which high-risk places you should avoid in your area
  • What you can do to keep yourself and others safe

Data we need

To give you tailored, up-to-date advice, this app will automatically:

  • Use your location
  • Access your Bluetooth

We need this information to see when you’re near other people using this app so that we can reduce the spread of coronavirus (COVID-19).

This information never leaves your phone.

You can remove the data at any time by deleting this app.
